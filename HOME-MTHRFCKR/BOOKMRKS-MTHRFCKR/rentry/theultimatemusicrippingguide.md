## Amazon Music Unlimited[](#amazon-music-unlimited)

---

*   Streaming/download audio quality and format:
    *   SD: OPUS variable (up to 320kbps)
    *   HD: FLAC 16-bit 44.1kHz
    *   UHD: FLAC up to 24-bit 192kHz
    *   Other: Dolby Atmos (e-ac3 JOC 768kbps 48kHz and/or AC-4 IMS), 360 Audio (MPEG-3D mha1 and/or mhm1 48khz 600kbps - 1700kbps) & Spatial Audio (variant of Atmos or 360 Audio, not the same as Apple)
*   Notes:
    *   Amazon Music Unlimited must be available in your region for more than SD. Example: India up to SD.
    *   [Region availability list](https://music.amazon.com/help?nodeId=202204440)
    *   [Difference between Amazon Music Prime and Amazon Music Unlimited](https://www.amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=GW3PHAUCZM8L7W9L)
*   Available to rip:
    *   All
*   Tool & requirements:
    *   None, private only.

## Apple Music[](#apple-music)

---

*   Streaming/download audio quality and format:
    *   High Efficiency: AAC 64kbps
    *   High Quality: AAC 256kbps
    *   Lossless: ALAC up to 24-bit 48kHz
    *   Hi-Res Lossless: ALAC up to 24-bit 192kHz
    *   Others: Dolby Atmos (e-ac3 JOC 768kbps 48kHz) & Spatial Audio (Apple proprietary rendered AAC binaural mix)
*   Available to rip:
    *   AAC 256kbps
    *   Private for higher than AAC (Lossless, Hi-Res and Atmos)
    *   Spatial Audio not possible to rip, for now.
*   Tool & requirements:
    *   **All versions/forks of the below tool will stop working in 3 months because of new Apple updates**
    *   [https://github.com/glomatico/gamdl](https://github.com/glomatico/gamdl)
    *   Needed: Widevine CDM from rooted android phone
    *   New Apple update broke token (token.txt created when inserting cookies.txt), thus if you didn't have one before you will not be able to use this tool anymore!

## Tidal[](#tidal)

---

*   Streaming/download audio quality and format:
    *   Normal: AAC 160kbps
    *   Hi-Fi: FLAC 16 bit 44.1kHz
    *   Master: MQA (lossy) encoded FLAC up to 24-bit 96kHz
    *   Others: Dolby Atmos (e-ac3 JOC 768kbps 48kHz and/or AC-4 IMS) & 360 Audio (MPEG-3D mha1 and/or mhm1 48khz 600kbps - 1700kbps)
*   Available to rip:
    *   All
*   Notes:
    *   WARNING about Tidal's Master. MASTER tag is NOT a indication a album is hi-res! MASTER is a lossy encoding that is also present on regular 16 bit 44.1kHz (example: Wu-Tang's Enter the Wu-Tang 36 Chambers). As a result, please check your files using a spectral program (like Spek).
*   Tool & requirements:
    *   [https://github.com/Dniel97/RedSea](https://github.com/Dniel97/RedSea)
    *   [https://github.com/Dniel97/orpheusdl-tidal](https://github.com/Dniel97/orpheusdl-tidal)
