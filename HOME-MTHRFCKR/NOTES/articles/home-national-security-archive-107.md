## DNSA

[![dnsa](/sites/default/files/thumbnails/image/dnsa.jpg "dnsa")](/digital-national-security-archive)

The **[Digital National Security Archive](/digital-national-security-archive)** (DNSA) is an invaluable online collection of more than 100,000 declassified records documenting historic U.S. policy decisions.

Read the documents that shaped U.S. responses to the Cold War, the terrorist attacks of 9/11, nuclear weapons proliferation, and beyond.

Talk to your university or local librarian about getting a subscription!

[More >>](https://about.proquest.com/en/products-services/dnsa/ "More DNSA")
