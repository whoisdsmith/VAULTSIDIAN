## Retiring Internet Explorer

With this release (V6.0) we have officially decided to end support for Internet Explorer. We still fully support [Microsoft Edge](https://www.microsoft.com/en-au/windows/microsoft-edge), along with all other modern and standards compliant browsers ([Firefox](https://www.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/new/), [Chrome](https://www.google.com/chrome/), [Safari](https://www.apple.com/au/safari/), [Opera](https://www.opera.com/)).
