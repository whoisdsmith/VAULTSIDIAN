# Distributed Denial of Secrets

From Distributed Denial of Secrets

[![Ddosecrets logo g.jpg](/images/thumb/2/25/Ddosecrets_logo_g.jpg/300px-Ddosecrets_logo_g.jpg)](/wiki/File:Ddosecrets_logo_g.jpg)

[](/wiki/File:Ddosecrets_logo_g.jpg "Enlarge")

**Distributed Denial of Secrets is a journalist 501(c)(3) non-profit devoted to enabling the free transmission of data in the public interest.**

We aim to avoid political, corporate or personal leanings, to act as a beacon of available information. As a transparency collective, we don't support any cause, idea or message beyond ensuring that information is available to those who need it most—the people.

You can [read more about our collective](/wiki/About "About"), and our decision to [embrace all sources of information](/wiki/Category:Ransomware "Category:Ransomware"). At its core, however, our mission is simple:

_**Veritatem cognoscere ruat cælum et pereat mundus**_
