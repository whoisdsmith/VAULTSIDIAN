## Deposit

To upload a research output to the repository, select the type of output from the dropdown menu and choose ‘Deposit’

It may take us up to 5 working days to confirm receipt of Theses deposits. If you require urgent acknowledgment that we have received your deposit, please [Email Us](mailto: ubira@lists.bham.ac.uk) once you have completed your deposit.
