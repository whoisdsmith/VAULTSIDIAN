## [1\. Add a URL shortener action](https://zapier.com/help/create/format/shorten-urls-in-zaps#add-url-shortener-action)

-   In the Zap editor, click the **Action** step, or click the **plus + icon** to add a step to your Zap.
-   Search for and select **URL Shortener by Zapier**.
-   Click the **Action Event** dropdown menu and select **Shorten URL**.
-   Click **Continue**.

___

## [2\. Customize your URL](https://zapier.com/help/create/format/shorten-urls-in-zaps#customize-your-url)

-   Enter the **URL** that you want to shorten. Include _http://_ or _https://_ in the URL.
-   Click **Continue**.

___

## [3\. Test your URL shortener action](https://zapier.com/help/create/format/shorten-urls-in-zaps#test-your-url-shortener-action)

-   Click **Test & Continue**.
-   If the URL you entered is valid, the step will show it was successful. You can use this shortened URL in later Zap steps.
