## [1\. Add a Formatter step to your Zap](https://zapier.com/help/create/format/convert-line-items-into-text-strings#add-a-formatter-step-to-your-zap)

-   In the Zap editor, click the **Action** step, or click the **plus + icon** to add an action to your Zap.
-   Select **Format**.
-   Click the **Action Event** dropdown menu and select **Utilities**.
-   Click **Continue**.

___

## [2\. Customize the line-item to text transformation](https://zapier.com/help/create/format/convert-line-items-into-text-strings#customize-the-line-item-to-text-transformation)

-   In the _Set up action_ section, click the **Transform** dropdown menu and select **Line-item to Text**.
-   In the _Values_ section, click the **Input** field and select a **line item field** from a previous step.
-   In the **Separator** field, enter the **symbol** or **text** that separates each value in your list. If nothing is entered, the Zap will use a comma by default.
-   Click **Continue**.

___

## [3\. Test your line-item to text transformation](https://zapier.com/help/create/format/convert-line-items-into-text-strings#test-your-line-item-to-text-transformation)

-   Click **Test & Review**. If the transformation is successful, the line items will be converted into a separate field for each item as well as a text string of all line items, separated by commas.
