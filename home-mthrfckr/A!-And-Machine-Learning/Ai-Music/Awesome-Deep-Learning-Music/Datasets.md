# List Of Datasets

---

Please Refer To The List Of Useful Acronyms Used In Deep Learning And Music: [Acronyms.md](AI-Music/awesome-deep-learning-music-master/acronyms.md).


- [Acoustic Event](https://data.vision.ee.ethz.ch/cvl/ae_dataset/) - Acoustic Event Dataset Is A Collection Of Annotated Sound Events In Everyday Environments, Designed To Promote Research On Acoustic Event Detection And Classification.
- [LSDB](lsdb.flow-machines.com/) - A Database Of Symbolic Music Data Designed For Music Research And Music Information Retrieval.
- [Piano-midi.de](Piano-midi.de): A Dataset Of MIDI Recordings Of Classical Piano Music, Along With Corresponding Sheet Music And Analysis.
- [ADC2004](http://labrosa.ee.columbia.edu/projects/melody/) - ADC2004 Is A Dataset Containing Monophonic Audio Recordings Of Solo Violin Pieces For Use In Melody Transcription Research.
- [AudioSet](https://research.google.com/audioset/index.html) - AudioSet Is A Large-scale Dataset Of Manually Annotated Audio Events. It Is Intended For Research In Audio Event Recognition And Machine Learning.
- [Bach10](http://music.cs.northwestern.edu/data/Bach10.html) - Bach10 Is A Dataset Of 10 Chorale Pieces By J.S. Bach, Designed For Automatic Music Analysis And Computational Musicology Research.
- [Bach Corpus](http://musedata.org/) - The Bach Corpus Is A Collection Of MIDI Files Of The Complete Works Of J.S. Bach. It Is Designed For Computational Musicology Research And Music Analysis.
- [Ballroom](http://mtg.upf.edu/ismir2004/contest/tempoContest/node5.html) - The Ballroom Dataset Is A Collection Of  Ballroom Dance Music, Labeled With The Tempo And The Dance Style. It Is Intended For Research In Beat Tracking And Music Information Retrieval.
- [Beatles](http://isophonics.net/content/reference-annotations-beatles) - The Beatles Dataset Is A Collection Of Multi-track Recordings Of Songs By The Beatles, With Annotations For Melody, Harmony, And Rhythm. It Is Intended For Research In Music Analysis And Music Information Retrieval.
- [C4S](http://mmc.tudelft.nl/users/alessio-bazzica#C4S-dataset) - The C4S Dataset Is A Collection Of Audio Recordings Of Sound Scenes, Designed For Research In Audio Scene Analysis And Machine Learning.
- [CCMixter](https://members.loria.fr/ALiutkus/kam/) - CCMixter Is A Collection Of Freely Available Audio Samples, Licensed Under Creative Commons Licenses, For Use In Music Production And Remixing.
- [DadaGP](https://github.com/dada-bots/dadaGP) - The DadaGP Dataset Is A Collection Of Audio Clips Of Various Genres And Styles, Intended For Use In Training Machine Learning Models For Music Generation.
- [DSD100](http://sisec17.audiolabs-erlangen.de/#/dataset) - DSD100 Is A Dataset Of Multi-track Recordings Of Songs, Designed For Research In Music Source Separation And Audio Signal Processing.
- [Echo Nest Taste Profile Subset](https://labrosa.ee.columbia.edu/millionsong/tasteprofile) - The Echo Nest Taste Profile Subset Is A Collection Of User Preferences For Music, Obtained From The Million Song Dataset. It Is Intended For Research In Music Recommendation Systems And Music Information Retrieval.
- [Free Music Archive](http://freemusicarchive.org/) - The Free Music Archive Is A Curated Collection Of High-quality, Legal Audio Downloads. It Is Intended For Use In Music Production And Creative Projects.
- [GTzan](http://marsyas.info/downloads/datasets.html) - GTzan Is A Collection Of Audio Recordings Labeled With Their Genre, Intended For Research In Music Genre Classification And Music Information Retrieval.
- [HHDS](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/0B1zpiGdDzFNlbmJyYU1VVFR3OEE) - The Hip Hop Drum Samples (HHDS) Dataset Is A Collection Of Drum Samples And Loops, Intended For Use In
- [IKala](http://mac.citi.sinica.edu.tw/ikala/): A Dataset Of Pop Music Recordings, With Annotations For Tasks Such As Singer Identification And Melody Extraction.
- [Jamendo](https://www.jamendo.com/start) - Jamendo Is A Platform That Offers Free, Legal And Unlimited Music Streaming And Downloads For Personal And Commercial Use. It Provides A Collection Of Over 500,000 Songs From Independent Artists Around The World.
- [Jamendo](http://www.mathieuramona.com/wp/data/jamendo/) - A Platform For Free And Legal Music Downloads Under Creative Commons Licenses.
- [Jazzomat Research Project](http://www.jazzomat.hfm-weimar.de/) - Jazzomat Is A Research Project That Focuses On Developing Algorithms And Tools For Analyzing And Generating Jazz Music. The Project Provides A Dataset Of Jazz Music Recordings And Annotations, As Well As Software Tools For Music Analysis And Generation.
- [J.S. Bach Chorales Dataset](https://github.com/czhuang/JSB-Chorales-dataset) - A Dataset Consisting Of 382 Four-part Harmonized Chorales Composed By J.S. Bach.
- [JSB Chorales](https://github.com/czhuang/JSB-Chorales-dataset) - A Dataset Containing 382 Four-part Harmonized Chorales Composed By J.S. Bach.
- [LabROSA](http://labrosa.ee.columbia.edu/projects/melody/) - A Project At Columbia University Dedicated To The Study Of Music And Audio Processing.
- [Lakh MIDI Dataset](https://colinraffel.com/projects/lmd/) - The Lakh MIDI Dataset Is A Collection Of 176,581 MIDI Files, Covering A Wide Range Of Music Genres And Styles. The Dataset Was Created By Crawling And Processing MIDI Files Available On The Internet, And It Is Designed For Research Purposes In The Field Of Music Information Retrieval.
- [Lakh MIDI](https://labrosa.ee.columbia.edu/sounds/music/) - A Collection Of Over 176,000 Unique MIDI Files.
- [Lakh Pianoroll Dataset](https://github.com/salu133445/musegan/blob/master/docs/dataset.md) - A Collection Of Over 174,000 Piano-rolls Extracted From Lakh MIDI Dataset.
- [Last.fm](https://www.last.fm/) - A Music Recommendation Service That Uses A User's Listening History To Generate Personalized Recommendations.
- [LMD](https://sites.google.com/site/carlossillajr/resources/the-latin-music-database-lmd) - A Dataset Containing Latin Music Recordings With Annotations.
- [LyricFind](http://lyricfind.com/) - A Platform That Provides Licensed Lyrics Content To Digital Music Services And Publishers.
- [MAESTRO](https://magenta.tensorflow.org/datasets/maestro/) - A Dataset Containing Over 200 Hours Of Virtuosic Piano Performances Captured With Fine Alignment (~3 Ms) Between MIDI And Audio.
- [Magnatagatune](http://mirg.city.ac.uk/codeapps/the-magnatagatune-dataset) - A Dataset Consisting Of 25,863 Mp3 Clips Of 30 Seconds Each, Each Annotated With Tags.
- [MagnaTagATune](http://mirg.city.ac.uk/codeapps/the-magnatagatune-dataset) - MagnaTagATune Is A Dataset Of 25,863 Music Clips, Each Labeled With Multiple Tags That Describe The Music's Genre, Mood, Instrumentation, And Other Features. The Dataset Was Created By Collecting Music Clips From The Internet And Crowdsourcing The Tagging Process.
- [MAPS](http://www.tsi.telecom-paristech.fr/aao/en/2010/07/08/maps-database-a-piano-database-for-multipitch-estimation-and-automatic-transcription-of-music/) - A Piano Database For Multipitch Estimation And Automatic Transcription Of Music.
- [MedleyDB](http://medleydb.weebly.com/) - A Dataset Of Annotated, Multitrack Recordings Of Popular Music Suitable For Research On Melody Extraction, Multi-instrument Detection, And Musical Source Separation.
- [MedleyDB](http://medleydb.weebly.com/) - MedleyDB Is A Dataset Of Multitrack Recordings Of Music Performances, Designed For Research In Music Analysis, Source Separation, And Other Related Fields. The Dataset Contains 122 Audio Recordings, Each With Separate Stems For Each Instrument Or Vocal Track.
- [Million Song Dataset](https://labrosa.ee.columbia.edu/millionsong/) - The Million Song Dataset Is A Collection Of Audio Features And Metadata For Over A Million Songs. The Dataset Was Created By Analyzing Audio And Metadata From Various Sources, Including The Echo Nest API And The Last.fm API. It Is Designed For Research In Music Information Retrieval, Music Recommendation, And Other Related Fields.
- [MIR-1K](https://sites.google.com/site/unvoicedsoundseparation/mir-1k) - A Dataset Of Musical Instrument Recordings And Corresponding Annotations For Sound Source Separation Research.
- [MSD](https://labrosa.ee.columbia.edu/millionsong/) - The Million Song Dataset, A Collection Of Audio Features And Metadata For A Million Contemporary Popular Music Tracks.
- [Musical Audio Dataset](https://sites.google.com/site/unvoicedsoundseparation/musdb) - The Musical Audio Dataset Is A Collection Of Multitrack Music Recordings, Designed For Research In Music Source Separation And Related Fields. The Dataset Contains 150 Songs From Various Genres And Styles, Each With Separate Stems For Each Instrument Or Vocal Track.
- [MusicNet](https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~thickstn/musicnet.html) - A Curated Dataset Of Classical Music Featuring Aligned Audio Recordings And Symbolic Music Data.
- [Nottingham Dataset](http://abc.sourceforge.net/NMD/): A Collection Of Traditional Folk Tunes In ABC Notation, A Text-based Music Notation Format.
- [NSynth Dataset](https://magenta.tensorflow.org/datasets/nsynth) - The NSynth Dataset Is A Collection Of Audio Recordings Of Musical Notes, Generated By A Neural Network Trained On A Large Collection Of Acoustic And Electronic Instrument Sounds. The Dataset Contains 305,979 Notes, Each With Associated Metadata Describing The Instrument, Pitch, And Other Features.
- [NTT MLS](http://www.ntt-at.com/product/speech/): A Dataset Of Speech Recordings From Various Speakers And In Multiple Languages, Including English, Japanese, And Mandarin.
- [Open Multitrack Testbed](http://www.semanticaudio.co.uk/projects/omtb/): A Dataset Of Multitrack Recordings Of Various Genres, Designed To Evaluate Source Separation And Other Audio Processing Algorithms.
- [Piano-e-Competition Dataset (Competition History)](http://www.piano-e-competition.com/): A Collection Of Recordings From The Participants Of The Annual Piano-e-Competition.
- [RWC](https://staff.aist.go.jp/m.goto/RWC-MDB/): A Collection Of Various Music Genres, Including Classical, Pop, And Folk, With Annotations For Various Tasks Such As Structural Analysis And Genre Classification.
- [RWC Music Database](http://staff.aist.go.jp/m.goto/RWC-MDB/) - The RWC Music Database Is A Collection Of Classical Music Recordings, Designed For Research In Music Information Retrieval And Related Fields. The Database Contains 3,129 Audio Recordings Of Classical Music Performances, Each With Associated Metadata Describing The Composer, Performer, And Other Features.
- [SALAMI](http://ddmal.music.mcgill.ca/research/salami/annotations): A Dataset Of Annotated Structural Analyses For A Diverse Collection Of Music Genres.
- [Speech Commands Dataset](https://ai.googleblog.com/2017/08/launching-speech-commands-dataset.html) - The Speech Commands Dataset Is A Collection Of Audio Recordings Of Spoken Words, Designed For Research In Speech Recognition And Related Fields. The Dataset Contains Over 100,000 Utterances Of 35 Words, Spoken By Thousands Of People. The Words Include Common Commands Such As "Stop," "Go," And "Yes," As Well As More Specific Words Such As "On," "Off," And "Down."
- [Symbolic Music Data](http://users.cecs.anu.edu.au/~christian.walder/): A Collection Of Symbolic Music Data In MIDI Format, Including Classical Music And Folk Tunes.
- [TIMIT](https://catalog.ldc.upenn.edu/LDC93S1): A Dataset Of Speech Recordings Of Various Dialects Of English, Designed For Speech Recognition Research.
- [TSP](http://www-mmsp.ece.mcgill.ca/Documents/Data/): A Collection Of Speech Datasets, Including Speaker Identification, Speech Recognition, And Speech Synthesis Tasks.
- [TUT Acoustic Scenes 2016](http://www.cs.tut.fi/~mesaros/pubs/mesaros_eusipco2016-dcase.pdf): A Dataset Of Environmental Sounds, Designed For Acoustic Scene Classification Research.
- [UIOWA MIS](http://theremin.music.uiowa.edu/mis.html): A Collection Of Electronic Music Recordings And Accompanying Metadata, Primarily Focusing On The Music Of The Late 20th Century.
- [US Pop](https://labrosa.ee.columbia.edu/projects/musicsim/uspop2002.html): A Collection Of Pop Music Recordings From The Early 2000s, With Annotations For Various Tasks Such As Genre Classification And Music Similarity.
